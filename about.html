About EASTCSO

The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre Students Organization (EASTCSO) is the official student body that represents all students of the institution. The organization promotes academic excellence, leadership development, innovation, student welfare, unity and effective communication between students and the administration.

Our Mission

To promote student welfare, leadership development, academic excellence, innovation and effective communication while empowering students to become responsible leaders.

Our Vision

To become a vibrant and inclusive students organization that inspires leadership, integrity, teamwork and excellence among all EASTC students.

Our Objectives

  • Represent students' interests.
  • Promote leadership and responsibility.
  • Organize academic, cultural and social activities.
  • Strengthen communication between students and management.
  • Encourage innovation, teamwork and integrity.
